Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new organization? +

To add a new organization, navigate to the Organizations page and click the "Add Organization" button. Follow the step-by-step wizard to configure basic information, technical setup, and domain settings.

How do I configure custom domains? +

Domain configuration is handled in the Domains section. You'll need to verify DNS ownership and configure SSL certificates. Our system provides automated DNS checks and certificate management.

What are the security requirements? +

The platform enforces HIPAA compliance with encryption at rest and in transit, multi-factor authentication, audit logging, and role-based access controls. Password policies and session management can be configured in Settings.
